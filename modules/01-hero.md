## Module 1：H1 + 首屏

**执行前必做**：拿到阶段 0.7 的「竞品 brief」。本模块的 Hero 段是用户阅读产品页的第一印象，**最容易模板化**，必须吃 brief 做差异化。

```
You are writing the H1 and hero section of a product page for [品牌名简称]'s [产品名].

Target reader: [目标读者]

Competitor brief (from 阶段 0.7):
- Differentiation lock (1-2 angles): [本篇主推角度]
- Hero phrasings competitors all use (MUST AVOID copying): [被讲烂的开头句式列表]

═══════════════════════════
H1
═══════════════════════════

Write the H1 heading. Format reference:
"Rod Mill Manufacturer & Supplier — Efficient Grinding Solutions for Mining"

Rules:
- Get to the point. No "Welcome to..." preamble.
- Include core keyword + value modifier
- 8-15 words
- Format: [Product Name] Manufacturer & Supplier — [Value/Application Modifier]

═══════════════════════════
Hero Paragraph — 新结构（定义 → 特点 → 功能 → 解决方案）
═══════════════════════════

Write a 3-4 sentence overview paragraph (70-105 words total) following EXACTLY this structure:

Sentence 1 — 产品基本定义 (~18-28 words):
- Define what this equipment IS (industry-engineer style, not marketing)
- Its position in the process / what category of equipment it belongs to
- ⚠️ DO NOT mention the brand name in this sentence. Start with the product, not the company.
- Reference openings:
  • "An impact crusher is a secondary/tertiary crushing machine that uses high-speed rotor and blow bars to reduce..."
  • "A rod mill is a tumbling grinding machine that uses long steel rods for coarse grinding in mineral processing."
  • "A trommel screen is a cylindrical rotary screen for separating bulk material by size in mining, aggregate, and recycling..."

Sentence 2 — 核心特点 (~18-25 words):
- 2-3 specific technical features (NOT marketing adjectives)
- Examples: "heavy-duty rotor / hydraulic gap adjustment / high-chrome blow bars" / "uniform particle size, stable performance, long service life"
- ⚠️ DO NOT start with "Our [product]" or "[Brand]'s [product]". Start with "Featuring..." / "With..." / "Built with..." / "Equipped with..."

Sentence 3 — 关键功能/产出 (~15-25 words):
- What the equipment DELIVERS (output result, what it achieves)
- Examples: "delivering excellent cubic particle shape, high reduction ratios, and consistent output gradation"
- "achieving uniform discharge size and high throughput across hard and abrasive materials"

Sentence 4 — 我们能提供的解决方案 (~18-30 words):
- The ONE sentence where brand name appears (or skip the brand name entirely if Sentence 1-3 already feel strong)
- Cover: scope (equipment + tailored config / service) + scope-of-application materials list
- Reference openings:
  • "[品牌名简称] supplies reliable [产品名] and tailored configurations for [materials] across [regions/industries]."
  • "Suitable for crushing [materials list], [品牌名简称] provides equipment and engineered solutions to customers worldwide."

═══════════════════════════
⚠️ 首段品牌词硬限制（绝对不能违反）
═══════════════════════════

- 整个 Hero Paragraph 内，品牌词（简称如 "Zhongyi"、全称、"Our [product]"、所有格 "[Brand]'s"）**总出现次数 ≤ 1**
- 推荐 0 次或 1 次。1 次时**只在 Sentence 4** 出现，Sentence 1-3 不允许任何品牌词
- 自查：写完数一遍 "Zhongyi" + "Our" + "[Brand]'s" 总数。≥ 2 立刻重写
- ❌ 违反案例（旧模板，不要照抄）：
  "Zhongyi is a professional European impact crusher manufacturer... Our European impact crushers feature... Zhongyi provides reliable equipment..."
  ↑ 3 次品牌词，严重违规

新结构 Format Reference（按定义→特点→功能→解决方案）
═══════════════════════════

✅ RECOMMENDED — Impact Crusher (4 sentences, brand mentioned once at end):
"An impact crusher is a secondary and tertiary crushing machine that uses a high-speed rotor and blow bars to reduce medium-hard to hard materials such as limestone, granite, basalt, and recycled concrete. Featuring a heavy-duty rotor, hydraulic gap adjustment, and high-chrome blow bars, it handles abrasive feeds with stable performance and long wear-part life. The design delivers excellent cubic particle shape, high reduction ratios, and consistent output gradation suited to quarries, mining, and recycling. Zhongyi supplies impact crushers and tailored configurations for customers across [regions], with engineering support from material analysis to commissioning."

✅ RECOMMENDED — Rod Mill (4 sentences, brand mentioned once at end):
"A rod mill is a tumbling grinding machine that uses long steel rods to deliver controlled coarse grinding in mineral processing, typically installed between primary crushing and downstream beneficiation. Built with a heavy-duty cylindrical shell, replaceable wear liners, and a low-speed drive, it produces uniform particle size with predictable energy consumption. The line-contact grinding action reduces over-grinding compared with ball mills, making it well-suited to quartz, feldspar, silica sand, and various metal ores. Zhongyi offers rod mills and engineered solutions for customers worldwide, from process design to wear-parts supply."

✅ RECOMMENDED — Roller Screen (3 sentences, brand mentioned ZERO times — also valid):
"A roller screen is a high-throughput screening machine that uses parallel rotating rollers to separate bulk material by size, commonly positioned ahead of primary crushers in mining, aggregate, and recycling lines. Featuring adjustable roller gaps (20–300 mm), anti-wrap profiles, and a tracked or stationary chassis, it handles wet and sticky feeds up to 15–20% moisture without blinding — a clear advantage over vibrating screens. The system delivers stable scalping efficiency on clay-bound and oversize feeds, with capacities reaching 500–800 t/h depending on roller width and gap."

❌ FORBIDDEN — 旧模板风格（绝不要再写成这样）：
"Zhongyi is a trusted [产品名] manufacturer and supplier, providing efficient solutions for [industry]. Our [产品名] deliver uniform particle size... Zhongyi offers reliable equipment to customers worldwide."
```

**执行说明**：
- 首屏 paragraph 用品牌简称（如 Zhongyi），不要用全称
- 参数值要合理范围，不能太整（如不要写 100, 200, 300, 400 这种）
- CTA 按钮文案二选一即可，不要给两个
- ⚠️ **生成后必须自查**：数一下 Hero Paragraph 内 "Zhongyi" + "Our" + "[品牌]'s" 的总出现次数，**≥2 立即重写**
- ⚠️ **结构自查**：Sentence 1 是否产品定义（不含品牌词）？Sentence 2 是否特点（不以 Our 开头）？Sentence 3 是否功能/产出？Sentence 4 是否解决方案？任一项不符立刻重写
